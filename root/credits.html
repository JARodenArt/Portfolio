Buckaroo Banzai Against the World Crime League Credits

2022

Blender, AfterEffects

I was tasked with taking a book or movie and creating the opening credits for a fictional movie adaptation. I chose to base mine off the unreleased sequel to The Adventures of Buckaroo Banzai Across the 8th Dimension. I created my version of the titular character's rocket car and had it racing across the desert while my fictional cast list appeared and the screen.
