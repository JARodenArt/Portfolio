Oswego Seasons Greeting Card

2022

Blender, Photoshop, AfterEffects

I was granted the opportunity to potentially have my version of SUNY Oswego's 2022 Season's Greeting card picked. I created mine by modeling SUNY Oswego's Sheldon Hall in Blender and bringing that into After Effects to add the snow and text.
