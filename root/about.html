    • Jacob Roden

    I'm from Weedsport, a small town in Central New York. I've been interested in 80s movies ever since I can remember which has influenced my art and hobbies, as I collect VHS tapes.

    Artist Statement

    Ever since the first time seeing computer-generated images used for special effects and animations in movies I knew I wanted to be a designer. I wasn’t able to work in 3D programs until high school, once I did I went beyond the class and did a lot of self-study. I create 3D models and use illustrations as a means of support by showing my design process. I like to take influences from different decades and use current styles and techniques to create interesting scenes.
